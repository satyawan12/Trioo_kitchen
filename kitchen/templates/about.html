About Trioo

Premium Stainless Steel Kitchens, Designed for Life

Who We Are

At Trioo Modular Kitchens, we craft stainless steel kitchens that blend durability, hygiene, and style. Our designs reflect modern tastes while staying rooted in utility — making us a trusted name in premium kitchen manufacturing.

Our Journey

Founded in 2015, Trioo set out to revolutionize kitchen design with stainless steel. We opened our first showroom in 2017, grew to 10+ cities by 2020, and expanded globally by 2023. Today, our legacy continues — driven by design, quality, and innovation.
